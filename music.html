OUR SOUND SYSTEM

A6TH SOUNDGEAR is more than just a sound system; it's the heartbeat of our band's sonic journey. With the latest, top-of-the-line equipment from around the globe, our sound system delivers crystal-clear audio that sets the stage on fire. What makes our sound truly unique is the expertise of our sound engineer, Susith Prasanna. With a wealth of experience working alongside some of Sri Lanka's biggest names in music, including Black, Nadeeka Guruge, Priya Sooriyasena, W.D. Amaradewa, Lasantha Kariyawasam, Kanchana Sitar, Sampath Anglia, Nirosha Virajini, Senanayaka Weraliyadda, Athula Adikari, Rookantha, and many more, Susith ensures that every performance is a sonic masterpiece. From our newly updated digital audio console to our cutting-edge line array system, A6TH SOUNDGEAR guarantees an unforgettable auditory experience that leaves audiences wanting more.
