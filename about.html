About A6th Band SL

A6TH, founded in 2019 by Sithija Miranda, has evolved from its humble beginnings as a wedding band to become a versatile musical ensemble renowned for its electrifying performances across Sri Lanka. Weathering the challenges posed by the pandemic, the band underwent a revitalization in 2023, infusing new energy and talent into its lineup. With a repertoire spanning Sinhala, English, pop, rock, jazz, modern, and classic genres, A6TH captivates audiences at weddings, corporate events, private functions, and popular pubs alike. What sets A6TH apart is not just their exceptional musical prowess, but also their unwavering commitment to delivering unforgettable experiences for their audiences. Equipped with their own professional sound system and a talented team of sound engineers, A6TH ensures that every performance is not only musically captivating but also acoustically impeccable, making them a standout presence in Sri Lanka's vibrant music scene

SITHIJA MIRANDA

Sithija Miranda, the visionary leader and owner of A6TH, commands the
stage with his rhythmic mastery as the band's bassist Having honed his skills alongside esteemed musicians like Marlon Amarasekara (Omega Band), Sithija's journey into music was not merely a passion but a destiny waiting to unfold. Formerly a banker, Sithija made the bold decision to pursue his musical calling, fueled by a legacy of sound engineering and recording prowess inherited from his father, a seasoned professional in the industry. Immersed in the vibrant music scene of Sri Lanka, Sithija has had the privilege of collaborating and drawing inspiration from icons such as Nadeeka Guruge, Sampath Anglia (2Forty2), Sumith Priyankara, Kanchana Sitar, and Ernie Francis (Naada Band), enriching his musical
palette and infusing A6TH's performances with a depth of experience and soulful expression.

RAVIN RATHNASIRI

mesmerizes audiences with his dynamic range and captivating stage presence.
A standout participant of 'The Voice Sri Lanka', Ravin's vocal prowess knows no bounds as he effortlessly transitions between English,
Sinhala, rock, classical, and more, infusing each performance with raw emotion and soulful expression.
Despite his legal background, Ravin's passion for music led him to pursue his calling as a full-time musician, foregoing his career as a lawyer.
His talent has garnered him opportunities to collaborate with esteemed bands such as Cold Sweat and Summer
Breeze, further cementing his reputation as one of Sri Lanka's most
versatile and sought-after vocalists.

SHAKYA SILVA

Shakya Silva, the heartbeat of A6TH as their versatile drummer, brings an unparalleled energy and precision to the band's performances.
With a natural talent that knows no bounds, Shakya effortlessly navigates through various musical genres,
infusing each beat with passion and flair.
His extensive experience includes collaborations with esteemed bands
such as Ohme, Summer Breeze, and Chain, showcasing his adaptability and skill across Sri Lanka's vibrant music scene.
Hailing from a lineage of musical excellence, Shakya inherits his talent from his father, the renowned drummer Heshan Silva, formerly of the iconic C Plus Band, ensuring that rhythm runs deep in his veins and resonates powerfully in
every note played with A6TH.

TREVEEN KAUSHALYA

Treveen Kaushalya, the youthful virtuoso on keys within A6TH, brings a modern edge and innovative flair to the band's sonic landscape.
As the youngest member, Treveen's boundless talent and mastery of high technology set him apart,
allowing him to craft unique tones that elevate the band's performances to new heights.
With a versatility that knows no bounds, Treveen seamlessly navigates through various musical genres, infusing each composition with his own distinctive touch.
His impressive resume includes collaborations with numerous choirs across Sri Lanka, as well as invitations from renowned bands within the country.
Yet, his heart remains with A6TH,
where his passion for music finds its truest expression, enriching every performance with his youthful energy and
creative ingenuity.

POORNA HASARANGA

Poorna Hasaranga, the maestro of the strings as A6TH's lead guitarist, infuses the band's sound with electrifying riffs and soul-stirring melodies.
With a deep-rooted love for rock music and an unparalleled versatility, Poorna effortlessly navigates through various musical styles, enriching each composition with his signature style.
His dedication to his craft is evident in his pursuit of knowledge, having studied studio mixing and mastering under the guidance of renowned expert Vinod Melpitiya at Ear Gear.
Drawing inspiration from his musical upbringing, inherited from his father and friends, Poorna's passion for music runs deep. Despite receiving invitations from numerous bands, including Line One and Chain, Poorna's heart lies with A6TH, where his guitar prowess finds its truest expression, igniting the stage with his fiery solos and infectious energy.
